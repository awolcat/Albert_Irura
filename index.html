  • Exchange [Currency Exchange Platform]

    Exchange is a platform that allows people travelling to Kenya to use their foreign-issued debit or credit card to credit their M-Pesa wallet. M-Pesa is a mobile-network based wallet that is widely used in Kenya and East Africa for day-to-day transactions like grocery shopping, utility bills, and peer-to-peer payments. Exchange leverages the convenince of M-Pesa and offers travellers easy access to the local currency through a short three step process and easy to use interface!
